Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how DIGIFERATE CONSULTING (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED ("we", "our", or "Service Provider") collects, uses, and protects information when you use HyperDOOH, including both the Android Mobile Application and the Website.

Section 1: Android Mobile Application

Permissions Used

The HyperDOOH Android Application uses the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)

The camera is used exclusively for computer vision and audience analytics purposes, including detecting general demographic attributes, motion, engagement levels, and viewing patterns.

The Application does NOT collect or store:

  • Personal identity information
  • Biometric identifiers
  • Facial recognition data
  • Personal images or video recordings

Camera processing is performed securely and only for real-time analysis purposes.

This data is used only to target the right content to the right people and is not shared or used for any other purposes.

---

Information Collected

The Application may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Application usage data
  • Session duration
  • Anonymous engagement metrics

This information is anonymized and used only to improve service functionality and performance.

---

Data Storage and Security

The Application does not store personal images, biometric identifiers, or personally identifiable information on servers or cloud storage.

All collected data is anonymized and secured using industry-standard protection mechanisms.

---

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Only anonymized and aggregated analytics data may be used internally to improve service quality.

---

User Control

Users may stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application.
